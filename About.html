About Nz World Travels Sdn Bhd

Nz World Travel is an award winning travel organization and excels with quality services from its experienced staffs. Founded 2014, NZ world travel was born out of a shared love for travel and a desire to redefine the way people experience the world. Over the years the company has received positive feedback from its existing clients and hence one can expect a cordial reception.

Our Vision

To be leaders of multi-national Airlines and travel management in the region and our client's best partner, always providing the best possible product, with the highest quality of services and demonstrating faithfully our commitment towards social and environmental responsibility.

Our Mission

Nz World Travel is committed to providing our clients with exceptional service. We aim to inspire and enable exploration by curating journeys that are personalized, enriching, and unforgettable. Through our expertise, passion, and dedication, we aim to make every trip a journey of a lifetime.
